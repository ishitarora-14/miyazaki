PLOT

Kiki, a 13-year-old witch-in-training, leaves home on a year-long journey to find out what it means to be a witch. She flies on her broomstick to the seaside town of Koriko, where she sets up a delivery service for the local residents. She soon makes friends with Osono, a local baker, and Ursula, a young witch who lives in the forest. Kiki's life is full of adventure and fun, but she soon discovers that being a witch isn't as easy as she thought.

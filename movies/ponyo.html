Ponyo on the Cliff

Release date: 19 September 2008

Director: Hayao Miyazaki

Producer: Toshio Suzuki

Written by: Hayao Miyazaki

Starring: Yuria Nara, Hiroki Doi, Yūki Amami, Kazushige Nagashima, Tomoko Yamaguchi

Runtime: 101 minutes

CHARACTERS

Ponyo

Sosuke

Fujimoto

Lisa

PLOT

The story begins with a powerful storm hitting a small fishing town, and during the storm, a young boy named Sosuke finds a goldfish trapped in a jar. Sosuke frees the goldfish and names her Ponyo. As Ponyo and Sosuke spend time together, Ponyo begins to develop feelings for Sosuke and wishes to become human so she can be with him. Ponyo's father, Fujimoto, is a powerful wizard who lives in the ocean and is determined to keep Ponyo from becoming human. However, Ponyo is determined to become a human and unleashes her magical powers, causing a great imbalance in the ocean's ecosystem and putting the entire town in danger. Sosuke and Ponyo embark on a dangerous journey to restore balance to the ocean and save the town. Along the way, they encounter various obstacles and magical creatures, including a giant wave and a mysterious sea goddess. In the end, Sosuke and Ponyo's love for each other prevails, and Ponyo becomes human. The town is saved, and the ocean's balance is restored. The movie ends with Sosuke and Ponyo living happily together as humans.

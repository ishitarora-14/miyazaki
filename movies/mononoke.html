PLOT

The movie begins with Ashitaka defending his village from a demon that has been rampaging through the countryside. During the battle, Ashitaka is infected with a curse that will slowly kill him unless he finds a way to lift it.

Ashitaka leaves his village in search of a cure and comes across a conflict between the humans of Iron Town and the forest gods, led by Princess Mononoke, a human raised by wolves. Lady Okkoto, a boar god, is also involved in the conflict as she is trying to protect the forest from the humans' destruction.

Ashitaka becomes a mediator between the two sides, trying to find a way for them to coexist peacefully. However, tensions rise as Lady Okkoto and Princess Mononoke become more determined to protect the forest, while Lady Eboshi, the leader of Iron Town, seeks to exploit its resources for profit.

As the conflict escalates, Ashitaka realizes that he must make a difficult choice between his loyalty to his people and his desire to protect the forest and its inhabitants. In the end, Ashitaka and Princess Mononoke work together to stop the destruction and find a way for humans and nature to coexist.

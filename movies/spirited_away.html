PLOT

Chihiro, a 10-year-old girl, is moving to a new home with her parents. On the way, they get lost and end up in an abandoned amusement park. Her parents are turned into pigs after eating the food meant for the spirits, and Chihiro finds herself trapped in a world of spirits. She discovers that the amusement park is a bathhouse for the spirits, where they come to relax and rejuvenate. The owner, Yubaba, takes Chihiro under her wing and gives her a job at the bathhouse to pay for her stay. As Chihiro tries to find a way to rescue her parents and escape from the spirit world, she meets a boy named Haku who helps her navigate the complex and dangerous world of the spirits. Together, they confront Yubaba and her minions, who try to keep Chihiro trapped in the spirit world forever. Through her journey, Chihiro learns important lessons about bravery, friendship, and perseverance. With the help of Haku and other friends she makes along the way, she eventually succeeds in rescuing her parents and returning to the human world, but not without experiencing unforgettable adventures and encountering strange and magical beings.

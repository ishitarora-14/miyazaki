PLOT

The movie begins with a young Jiro dreaming of flying planes and designing them. He is inspired by the famous Italian aeronautical designer Caproni, who appears to him in his dreams. As Jiro grows older, he attends university and becomes an accomplished aircraft designer. However, his dreams are overshadowed by the impending war, and he struggles with the moral implications of his work. Jiro meets and falls in love with a young woman named Nahoko, who becomes his muse and source of inspiration. Their love story unfolds amidst the turmoil of Japan's militarization and eventual involvement in World War II. As Jiro's designs become more advanced and the war intensifies, he begins to realize the devastating consequences of his work. The movie depicts his struggle with his conscience, as he comes to terms with the fact that his creations will be used for destruction. In the end, Jiro creates his masterpiece, the Mitsubishi A6M Zero, but realizes that it will be used as a weapon of war. He accepts the responsibility for his actions and acknowledges that he has contributed to the war effort, but also laments the loss of innocent lives.
